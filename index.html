Local businesses are the backbone of every neighborhood, yet they remain the hardest to find, the least supported, and the first to disappear.

Town Square is changing that. By combining hyperlocal discovery, real community voices, and direct connections between residents and the businesses they live alongside, we're building something that has never existed before: a digital town square where neighborhoods actually come alive.

This prototype is the first look at that vision, and we're looking for partners ready to help us bring it to every community that deserves it.
